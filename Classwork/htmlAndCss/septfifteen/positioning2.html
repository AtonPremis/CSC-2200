Braveheart is a 1995 epic film about the life of William Wallace a Scottish warrior who led a rebellion to free Scotland from English rule around the late 13th century. Directed by and starring Mel Gibson, the movie is known for its dramatic portrayal of Wallace's fight for freedom against King Edward I, its sweeping battles, and its romantic elements. While the film took significant liberties with historical facts for dramatic effect, it became a celebrated war epic and is considered an important cinematic work by many
