This text wraps around the tall red image. But look at the purple border - does it contain the floated image?

Floated elements are removed from normal document flow, so containers don't automatically expand to contain them.

Understanding Float:

  • float: left - Pushes element to left, other content wraps around right side
  • float: right - Pushes element to right, other content wraps around left side
  • Floated elements are removed from normal flow - Containers might not wrap around them
  • Fix with overflow: auto - Forces container to contain floated children
