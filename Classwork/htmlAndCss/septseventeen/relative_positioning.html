Problem 1: Position Relative Practice

First paragraph - I stay in my normal position

Second paragraph - Move me 20px right and 10px down!

Third paragraph - What happens to me when #special moves? This paragraph doesn't get affected

Your Tasks:

Task A: Add CSS to #special to move it 20px right and 10px down using position: relative

Task B: After you add the CSS, observe what happens to the space where "Second paragraph" normally would be. Does the third paragraph move up to fill the gap?

Remember: position: relative moves an element from where it would normally appear, but keeps its original space reserved.
