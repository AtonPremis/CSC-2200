Top 3 Cartoons

Here is my top 3 favorite Cartoons of all time.

SlugTerra

My favorite cartoon of the bunch, SlugTerra was my childhood cartoon. It has a unique power system in there is some slugs in the world of SlugTerra that has locked superpowers. The only way to unlock their superpowers is for them to travel very fast, and that is done using specialized guns, called blasters. Not only is the power system new, the characters are also written well. It does have some stereotype characters, like the big and muscular man, old and goofy person, and a smart and rational female. But, the chemistry between all these characters is really well done and feels like their family. For a cartoon, it has some really mature villains in it. Not all villains are evil just to be evil but actually has backstory and why they became who they are. Overall, all these qualities and some more made it my favorite cartoon.

My Little Pony

I know this is a pretty girlish cartoon especially to watch for a boy like me, but let me tell you, it is an awesome cartoon. I'll give an example of one of my favorite scenes in the cartoon. To start, Rainbow Dash, a pony with a dream of becoming the fastest pegasus, enters a race to decide the fastest pegasus. Things starts off simple, her and all other competitors are racing. But then, one of the racers got into a collision and is falling down from a very great height. Rainbow Dash sees this and has to decide, whether to try and save the racer or to continue with the race. Of course, she decides to try to save the racer. But, she isn't able to catch up fast enough. All hope seems to be lost, but then, Rainbow Dash breaks the sound barrier, with awesome rainbow trails behind her. She saves the racer, but lost the race in the process. While she has lost, people still cheer for her and have achieved something greater than her dream.

Wild Kratts

Wild Kratts follows the adventure of two brothers, Chris and Martin. There isn't a main villain, or at least I don't remember it, but each episode, there is a threat to the brothers. In order to overcome the threats, the brothers has to collect animal's DNA, and use the DNA to transform into that animal. When doing this, the brothers gets special attributes and abilities pertaining to the selected animal. Each episode's plot and story and always interesting, and the brothers has to use new animal powers to overcome the new threat, as using old powers don't always work. This cartoon also does a great job at informing the audience what the animals do and have in their toolbox to survive the wild. Personally, I got hooked into the cartoon because of the variety of animals and situations the characters were put in.
