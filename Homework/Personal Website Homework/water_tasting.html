My Water Tasting Rank

Criteria

Some things to consider when ranking bottled water is the taste and odor of the water. Some can taste too salty or have a beach like smell. Appearance also matters as is there a lot or little bubbles. Can we see any impurities in the water? The plastic container also counts. How grippy is the bottle is? How many ripples are there?
